Academic calender

At the beginning of every academic year, the University prepares an academic calendar meticulously. This academic calendar is placed before the Academic Council and Executive Council for their approval. Once approved, all the activities including teaching, tests and end-semester examination are conducted as per the academic calendar. The academic calendar is a ‘non-negotiable’ document. Over the years, the University has been conducting all the examinations as per the academic calendar and declaring the results within 45 days as per the norms without any hassle.
